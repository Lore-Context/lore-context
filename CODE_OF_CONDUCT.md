# Code Of Conduct

Lore Context contributors are expected to keep collaboration professional, precise, and
useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Protect private data, credentials, customer information, and security reports.
- Assume good intent while still checking claims carefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private credentials, customer data, or vulnerability details without
  permission.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or report abuse
to platform providers when needed to protect the project and its users.
